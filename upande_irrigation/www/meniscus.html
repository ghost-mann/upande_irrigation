Meniscus Guide

Weather Tab

Rainfall and ETo on a single chart for direct comparison. Temperature is a full-width envelope. Irrometer readings are shown as compact gauges grouped by section. The Farm filter scopes all weather data to a specific farm; "All Farms" combines them.

IoT Sensors Tab

Live telemetry from the Sensor Readings doctype via upande_irrigation.api.sensors.fetch. One card per device (deveui) with the latest value, units, sparkline, battery, RSSI, and SNR. Sensors silent for > 6 hours show a Stale badge.

Filter by sensor type (Soil Moisture, Reservoir Level, etc.). The date range from the topbar controls the lookback window for the readings table and sparklines.

Water & Energy Tab

Pulls from Water Meter Reading, Electricity Meter Reading, and Reservoir Pumping Record via upande_irrigation.api.resources.fetch.

Flow Diagram visualises river → pump → dam → sections. Monthly Bars show pump volume, electricity, and efficiency (m³/kWh) by month. Section Bars show proportional water use across the four irrigation sections.

API Scripts

Weather: upande_irrigation.api.weather.fetch
Resources: upande_irrigation.api.resources.fetch
Sensors: upande_irrigation.api.sensors.fetch

All accept a days parameter. Weather also accepts farm; resources also accepts section; sensors accept deveui, sensor_type, and sensor_name.
